About Sky Betting & Gaming

Our applications are written in all manner of languages, including Node.js, NextJS, PHP, Golang, and a few JVM-based variations. We utilise a number of open source projects including Kubernetes, MySQL, Redis, RabbitMQ, Kafka, and Prometheus. Our infrastructure is made up of over 4000 virtual and physical machines spread across five data centres and managed using Chef & Ansible, as well as extensive use of public and private cloud vendors managed using Terraform.
